Personal tech radar [2016-01]

I plan to do this exercice every 6 months to see how much my skills are growing.
It should also give me a push to learn more and faster.
Got the idea of a personal tech radar from http://nealford.com/memeagora/2013/05/28/build_your_own_technology_radar.html

I changed the definition of the 4 rings:

  • Adopted: Technologies & Techniques that I've worked with recently and became confident with.
  • Trying: Technologies & Techniques that I've tried a bit and want to gain more knowledge on.
  • Will try: Technologies & Techniques that I've not used/tried yet & want to take a more serious look in the next 6 months.
  • Hold: Technologies & Techniques that I would like to try in the future, but don't feel the need to look into in the next 6 months.
    Also may include technologies I have tried, but don't feel I would need them in my skillset now (or ever).
